Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2023

Permissions

We currently do not ask for any special permissions of your device such as GPS, camera, contacts, storage etc, because the App currently does not rely on them.

Data Safety

We are committed to protect your personal information and your right to privacy. We do not collect, store or share your personal information. We do not share your information with third-parties.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't track your activity or usage patterns
  • We don't collect your location data
  • We don't access your contacts or other personal information
  • We don't read or write to your device storage without permission
  • We don't use cookies or web beacons to track you
  • We don't share any data with third parties or advertisers

Third-Party Services

QuranApp doesn't integrate with any third-party services that could compromise your privacy. We don't include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks in our application.

About the Qur'an

The Noble Quran is the central religious text of Islam. Muslims believe the Qur'an is the book of Divine guidance and direction for mankind, and consider the original Arabic text the final revelation of Allah (God).

It is widely regarded as the finest work in classical Arabic literature. It is organized in 114 chapters (surah سورة), which consist of verses (ayah آية).

About QuranApp

The QuranApp is an ad-free and privacy-focused mobile application that facilitates exploring the Qur'an on daily basis at any time and place. Its easy-to-navigate and neat & clean design helps users to overcome the difficulties of reading the Qur'an in the time of technology.

The QuranApp focuses on the latest technologies to aim at providing comprehensive convenience to the users. This app has translations in several languages so that people of different languages and regions can understand the purpose of the Qur'an. All translations of the original Arabic text are thus interpretations of the original meanings and should be embraced as such. This app is licensed under GPL v3.0.
